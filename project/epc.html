TRANSCO CONTRACT N14512 - 132/22kV Substation at CUP2

END CLIENT : TRANSCO /ADDC/ ADAC

The scope of work includes Design, Supply, Installation, Testing & Commissioning of a new 132/22kV Substation at CUP2 for providing power reliability to the New Midfield Terminal in Abu Dhabi Airport. As part of scope, we supplied the following:

  • New Building to house all electrical and mechanical components along with access road
  • HVAC System
  • Fire Fighting System
  • 132kV Switchgear
  • 132/22kV Power Transformers
  • 22kV Switchgear
  • 22kV Capacitor Banks
  • Separate Substation Control & Monitoring System (SCMS) for 132kV System and 22kV System
  • 132kV Busbar Protection and Protection panels
  • 22kV Busbar Protection and Protection Panels
  • Fault Monitoring System (FMS)
  • LVAC System for TRANSCO & ADDC
  • DC System for TRANSCO & ADDC
  • Tariff Metering System
  • Telecommunication System
  • DMS Integration and LDC Integration Works

AADC CONTRACT A14861 - 33/11kV Substation at Al Ain Labour Camp & Associated Cabling Works

END CLIENT : AADC

The scope of work includes Design, Supply, Installation, Testing & Commissioning of a new 33/11V Substation at Al Ain Labour Camp. As part of scope, we supplied the following:

  • New Building to house all electrical and mechanical components along with access road
  • HVAC System
  • Fire Fighting System
  • 33kV Switchgear
  • 11kV Switchgear
  • 33/11kV Power Transformers
  • 33kV Capacitor Banks
  • 33kV & 11kV Busbar Protection and Protection panels
  • LVAC System
  • DC System
  • Tariff Metering System
  • DMS Integration
  • 33kV Power Cabling Works & FO Cabling Works(51Km)

AADC CONTRACT A14235.2 – Protection Relay & Busbar Protection System Replacement works in Legacy AADC existing Substations

END CLIENT : AADC

The scope of work includes replacement of Old Electromagnetic relays and Design, Supply, Installation, Testing & Commissioning of new numerical relays. As part of scope, we supplied the following:

  • 33kV Line differential relays
  • 33kV Transformer differential relays
  • 33kV Low Impedance Busbar Protection Panels
  • 33kV & 11kV Over Current & Backup Relays
